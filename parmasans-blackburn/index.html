Halal Parmasans Blackburn – Classic, Hot Shot, Donner, Pepperoni, Peri

A parmasan is breaded chicken with bechamel sauce and cheese. Rack City Kitchen makes a halal version with buttermilk chicken and a range of toppings.

Note

ALL PARMASANS SERVED WITH FRIED CHICKEN, SALAD, CREAMY CABBAGE, SKIN ON FRIES AND A DRINK.

Classic Parmo

£7.99

Buttermilk fried chicken butterfly coated with house special parmo sauce, topped with cheddar cheese, skin on fries.

Hot Shot Parmo

£8.49

Buttermilk fried butterfly chicken, house parmo sauce, onion, peppers, sweetcorn, jalapeno, crushed chilli flakes, topped with cheddar cheese, skin on fries.

Stir Fry Donner Parmo

£8.49

Buttermilk fried butterfly chicken, house parmo sauce, homemade stir fry donner, onions, peppers, topped with cheddar cheese, skin on fries.

Pepperoni Parmo

£8.49

Buttermilk fried butterfly chicken, house parmo sauce, pepperoni, peppers, jalapeno, crushed chilli flakes, topped with cheddar cheese, skin on fries.

Peri Peri Parmo

£9.49

Buttermilk fried butterfly chicken topped with parmo sauce, peri peri chicken, peppers, onions, sweetcorn, topped with cheddar cheese, skin on fries. Served with Rubicon Sparkling Mango drink.

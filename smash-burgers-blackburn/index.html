Smash Burgers Blackburn – Halal Angus Beef

A smash burger is a beef patty pressed thin on a hot grill. The pressure creates a crispy, caramelised crust. The centre stays juicy. Rack City Kitchen makes every smash burger with 100% halal Angus beef. No frozen patties. No shortcuts.

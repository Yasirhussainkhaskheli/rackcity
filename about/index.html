About Rack City Kitchen – Built for the Bold

Rack City Kitchen opened in Blackburn to serve a better kind of fast food. Fresh halal meat. Sauces made in house. No corners cut.

Written by the Rack City Kitchen team in Blackburn. Content reviewed and updated by the Rack City Kitchen team based in Blackburn.

Our signature is the smash burger – thin, crispy patty from Angus beef. We also serve buttermilk chicken burgers, stir fry donner, parmasans, loaded fries, fresh dough pizzas and shakes. Every item made fresh when you order.

Our Values

Fresh Daily – No frozen patties. No pre‑packed sauces.

100% Halal Certified – All meat sourced from approved halal suppliers.

Made to Order – Your food is cooked when you order. No heat lamps.

Local Business – Based in Blackburn. Deliver to Blackburn and surrounding areas.

Built to Scale – Designed for expansion. Brand, systems and recipes ready.
